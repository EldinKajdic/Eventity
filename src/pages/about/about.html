About

Eventity

The Eventity Program was started in 2012, following the rising numbers of robots buying tickets for events and retailing the purchased tickets for profit.

Who are we?

We strive to end the mass retailing of personal tickets and aim to lower the official price of concert tickets. The Eventity Group works as a non-profit organisation.

What do we do?

Originally, The Eventity Group was created as The Identity Group. The application was first designed to only create and read QR-codes to enable identifying of students easier. After a while, it shifted direction towards becoming an application for musical events. The Eventity Program was founded by Eldin Kajdic in 2017.
